About Once-A-Day!

Once-A-Day! is a daily micro-learning experience designed to make curiosity addictive.

Each day, you choose a topic and explore a small set of ideas: facts, vocabulary, and one mystery question ‚Äî just enough to spark wonder without overwhelm.

No feeds. No doomscrolling. Just one meaningful thought a day.

This project blends science, humanities, and playful design to help learning feel lightweight and rewarding.
